Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–3 working days (Monday–Friday, excluding UK bank holidays). During busy periods (sales, launches, holidays), processing times may be slightly longer. If there are delays, we’ll notify you by email.

Shipping Locations

We currently ship within the United Kingdom only. We’re working on adding international shipping in the future.

Delivery Times (UK)

Standard delivery usually takes 3–5 working days after dispatch. Delivery times are estimates only. Once your order has been handed to the courier, delays caused by the courier or Royal Mail are outside our control.

Shipping Fees

Shipping costs are shown at checkout before you confirm payment. Any promotional or free-shipping offers will be clearly displayed on our website or social media.

Order Confirmation & Tracking

After placing an order, you’ll receive an order confirmation email. Once your order is dispatched, you’ll receive a shipping confirmation email, which may include a tracking number or delivery reference (where available).

Wrong Address / Missed Delivery

Please double-check your address at checkout. We are not responsible for orders delivered to an incorrect address provided by the customer. If a parcel is returned to us due to an incorrect or incomplete address or missed delivery, we can resend it once additional postage has been paid.

Returns & Exchanges

Return Eligibility

You may return unused, unopened and sealed items within 14 days of delivery for an exchange or refund. For hygiene reasons, we cannot accept returns on opened or used lip products, lashes or press-on nails, unless the item is faulty or damaged.

Damaged or Faulty Items

If your item arrives damaged or faulty, please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order. Email: hello@batluxebeauty.com with:

  • Your name
  • Order number
  • Clear photos of the item and packaging

We’ll review your claim and offer a replacement, store credit or refund where appropriate.

Return Process

  1. Contact us at batluxebeauty@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return.
  2. If your return is accepted, we’ll send you the return address and instructions.
  3. Pack the item securely and post it back to us.

Unless the item is faulty, you are responsible for return postage costs. We recommend using a tracked service as we cannot be responsible for lost return parcels.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we’ll email you to confirm approval or rejection. Approved refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5–10 working days, depending on your bank. Shipping charges are usually non-refundable, except where we sent a faulty or incorrect item.

Exchanges

If you’d like to exchange an item and the product is unopened, please email us first. Exchanges are subject to stock availability.
