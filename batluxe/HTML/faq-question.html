Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused and unopened products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the US. We also offer expedited shipping options (2-3 business days) and overnight shipping for an additional fee.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes! All BatLuxe Beauty products are 100% cruelty-free. We never test on animals and are committed to ethical beauty practices.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship within the United States only. We're working on expanding our international shipping options and hope to serve more countries soon.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's website.
